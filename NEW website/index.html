Story

Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted young boy, lives a peaceful life with his family in the mountains. However, his world is shattered when he returns home one day to find his family slaughtered by demons, and his younger sister, Nezuko, is turned into a demon herself. Determined to protect her and find a cure, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, an elite group of warriors dedicated to exterminating demons and protecting humanity. Along the way, he befriends fellow slayers, including Zenitsu Agatsuma and Inosuke Hashibira, and begins his arduous journey to defeat the demons and save Nezuko.

As Tanjiro and his friends grow stronger, they face increasingly powerful demons, many of whom are part of the Twelve Kizuki, a group of elite demons who serve under the Demon King, Muzan Kibutsuji. Each battle brings new challenges and reveals the tragic pasts of the demons they fight, showing that not all demons are born evil. Despite the overwhelming odds, Tanjiro’s unwavering determination to protect his sister and bring an end to Muzan’s reign of terror keeps him moving forward. Alongside his comrades, he trains hard to master the Breath Styles and uncover the mysteries behind the demons' origins.

Throughout his journey, Tanjiro faces heartbreaking losses and struggles to hold onto his humanity, even as the world around him grows darker. His bond with Nezuko remains his greatest source of strength, as he refuses to give up on her, hoping that one day she will regain her human form. As the final battle against Muzan approaches, Tanjiro realizes that the fight against evil may come at a great personal cost. But with his courage, his friends, and his unbreakable will to protect those he loves, Tanjiro prepares to face the ultimate evil in a final showdown that will decide the fate of humanity.
